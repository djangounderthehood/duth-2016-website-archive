Django: Under The Hood

Django: Under the Hood is back for its third edition! Join us for this exciting Django conference full of deep, detailed talks and amazing opportunities to shape the future of Django.

The conference is being organised by members of the Django community, including several members of the Django core team, and in association with the Dutch Django Association. All attendees must follow the Code of Conduct.
