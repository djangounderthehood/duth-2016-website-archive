🔊 Django: Under The Hood line-up announced! 🔊

Published on 13th Jun 2016 by Ola Sitarska

We couldn't be more excited to reveal the topics that we will discuss in-depth in November this year with you!

It's finally here! Line-up for Django: Under The Hood 2016! This year, we are gonna explore following topics together with our fantastic speakers:

  • Andrew Godwin will speak about Django Channels
  • Ana Balica will speak about Testing
  • Aymeric Augustin will speak about Debugging
  • Nadia Eghbal will speak about Funding Open Source Software
  • Loïc Bistuer will speak about Validation
  • Idan Gazit will speak about JavaScript

We still have a couple of secrets to unveal in the next few weeks, so watch this space! 🚀
